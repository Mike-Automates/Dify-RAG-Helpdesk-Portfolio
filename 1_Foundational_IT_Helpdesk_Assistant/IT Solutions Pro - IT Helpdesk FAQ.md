# IT Solutions Pro - IT Helpdesk FAQ
  
This document covers common questions for employees at IT Solutions Pro.

---

### 1. How do I request a new peripheral (mouse, keyboard, monitor)?

* **Answer:** All requests for new hardware must be submitted through the IT Helpdesk Portal. Navigate to the "Hardware Request" section and fill out the form, providing a brief justification for the new equipment. Approvals are typically processed within 3-5 business days.

---

### 2. How do I reset my password?

* **Answer:** If you know your current password, you can change it at any time by pressing `Ctrl + Alt + Del` and selecting "Change a password." If you have forgotten your password, please use the "Forgot Password" link on the company login page. This will initiate a password reset email to your registered secondary email address.

---

### 3. What should I do if my internet connection is slow or not working?

* **Answer:** First, try restarting your computer and your router (if you have one). If the issue persists, check if other devices on the network are also experiencing slow speeds. If you are the only one affected, please run a speed test and report the results to the IT Helpdesk with a ticket.

---

### 4. How do I access the company's VPN from home?

* **Answer:** To connect to the corporate network from home, you need to install the **GlobalProtect** VPN client. The software can be downloaded from the "Remote Access" section of the IT Helpdesk Portal. Once installed, use your standard IT Solutions Pro credentials to log in.

---

### 5. How do I get software installed on my computer?

* **Answer:** To maintain system integrity and security, all software installations must be handled by the IT department. Please submit a request through the IT Helpdesk Portal, providing the name of the software and a brief explanation of why you need it.
